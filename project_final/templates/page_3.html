About Our Founder

Our founder, Dr. Maria Rodriguez, has always been passionate about immunology and the role it plays in human health. Growing up, she watched her older sister struggle with a severe autoimmune disorder and was inspired to learn more about the immune system and how it can go awry.

As a young scientist, Dr. Rodriguez dedicated herself to studying immunology and worked tirelessly to gain a deep understanding of the complex processes involved. She eventually earned her PhD in Immunology and went on to work at several top research institutions, making significant contributions to the field.

But Dr. Rodriguez always felt that she could do more to help people suffering from immunological disorders.
